# Privacy Policy for The Ruckus Comedy App

Last updated: May 2026

The Ruckus Comedy app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
We do not collect any personal information including name, email, location, or device identifiers.

## Contact Form
Messages submitted through the app's contact form are sent directly through your device's email application. We do not store or retain any messages on our servers.

## Third Party Services
The app links to third party streaming platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, iHeartRadio, and YouTube Music. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle your data.
